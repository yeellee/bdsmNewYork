Our Purpose

One of the most difficult aspects of traveling within the Big Apple is understanding its subway infrastructure. Though the city places a large emphasis on this means of public transportation, it is quite difficult to understand the maps provided within terminals. Moreover, these underground stations are predominantly out of reach of internet connections strong enough to open online maps. With BDSMnyc, navigating the routes of NYC will no longer be hard. By using its downloaded database, BDSMnyc is able to ask for position and wanted destination without the need of a wifi connection or cellular data.
